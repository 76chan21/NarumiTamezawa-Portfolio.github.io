Born in Atsugi, Kanagawa, and raised in Hachioji, Tokyo. I joined a video production club in high school wanting to create "cool dance videos", but ended up starting short film making under the influence of friends, then my directorial work won several awards. In university, I majored in film-based video production while gaining experience as an offline editor for music videos and web commercials at a video production company. I also worked as the filming and editing lead for the creative team "Tomoshibito," where I pursued the fusion of dance and video expression, contributing to the team's branding as well. Later, I moved to Toronto and majored in Interactive Media Design at college, where I developed knowledge in design, business, and web development. My works excel in visual storytelling, drawing from the rhythm I cultivated as a dancer and the "show, don't tell" approach I learned from filmmaking.
