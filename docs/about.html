Hello, I'm Narumi, a Visual Storyteller and Content Creator based in Toronto, Canada. With 6 years of experience in the video industry, I am currently pursuing Interactive Media Design at Seneca Polytechnic.

Born and raised in Japan, I started dancing at the age of four, which developed my strong sense of rhythm. My fascination with video began when I was captivated by the opening visuals at my favorite dance artist's concert. In high school, I began creating short films, and my directorial works received several awards. Entertaining people has always been second nature to me, and nothing brings me more joy than moving people's emotions and thoughts through my creations.

My strength lies in crafting visually engaging narratives with a rhythmic flow, combined with compelling storytelling skills honed through my filmmaking background. By studying Interactive Media Design, I have expanded my knowledge beyond video production to include Motion Design, Branding, and Strategic Design. This allows me to create more engaging and impactful works, supported by strategic and theoretical foundations.

Beyond the screen, I enjoy solving puzzles, playing board games, crafting, exploring new restaurants, and expressing my thoughts and experiences through writing. Recently, I've been balancing my creative pursuits with weekends filled with adventures with friends.
